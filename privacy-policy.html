This Privacy Policy describes how Penny Health Monitor ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our personal health monitoring application integrated with Oura Ring.

1. Information We Collect

Through the Oura API, we may access the following data with your explicit consent:

  • Sleep Data: Sleep stages, duration, and quality metrics
  • Activity Data: Daily activity, steps, and movement patterns
  • Heart Rate Data: Resting heart rate, HRV, and heart rate trends
  • SpO2 Data: Blood oxygen saturation levels
  • Readiness & Stress: Daily readiness scores and stress indicators
  • Workout Data: Exercise sessions and recovery metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your personal health metrics and trends
  • Generating insights to help you understand your wellness patterns
  • Personal health tracking and monitoring purposes only

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal secure storage
  • We do not share, sell, or transmit your health data to third parties
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication is used for secure API access
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Oura account settings

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as you actively use the application. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or revoking API access.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke consent and API access at any time

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the Oura Ring API. Please review Oura's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
